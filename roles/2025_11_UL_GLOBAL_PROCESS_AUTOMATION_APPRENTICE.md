# UL - Global Process Automation Apprentice

As the DPI Global Process Automation Apprentice on the Intelligence Automation team, the individual will have the exciting opportunity to contribute to the development of robotic process automation solutions while learning and growing in a dynamic environment.

## Core responsibilities and competencies required
- Conduct thorough process analysis and requirements gathering
- Create comprehensive requirements and test case documentation 
- Participate in User Acceptance Testing
- Interview process owners and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) to collect detailed business process information
- Collaborate with stakeholders to assess current state processes
- Engage in huddles and follow Agile development methodologies
- Analyze and document business processes at a keystroke level
- Learn the capabilities and limitations of robotic process automation software tools
- Observe and analyze processes to understand steps, interdependencies, and applications used in automation
- Communicate effectively between business process owners and developers to ensure clear understanding and successful project implementation 

## Essential technical skills

- Technical writing experience

- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

- Strong attention to detail and analytical thinking
- Eagerness to learn and adapt to new technologies and methodologies
